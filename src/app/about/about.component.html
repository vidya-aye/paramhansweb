About Us

Our Legacy

श्री श्री १०८ श्री राम कृपाल दास परमहंस आश्रम (गृहस्थ) is a spiritual place dedicated to the teachings of Bhagwan Shiv Shankar ji . The Ashram is a sanctuary for those seeking peace, spiritual wisdom, and guidance to lead a righteous life. With deep roots in the traditional values of Indian spirituality, the Ashram has been a beacon of hope for many devotees.

Founded by Shri राम कृपाल दास परमहंस जी, the Ashram focuses on spreading the message of love, compassion, and devotion to the divine. It is a place where individuals of all backgrounds come together to practice spirituality and contribute to the well-being of society through selfless service (seva) and spiritual practice.

Our Vision

Our vision is to create a peaceful and harmonious world where individuals are connected to their inner selves, cultivating love, compassion, and understanding. Through the teachings of Shri Ram and the blessings of Shri राम कृपाल दास परमहंस जी, we aim to lead individuals towards the ultimate truth and spiritual awakening.

Our Mission

Our mission is to provide a space for individuals to experience true spiritual growth. The Ashram is dedicated to promoting the principles of dharma, selfless service, and devotion. We organize spiritual programs, satsangs, and community service projects that help people on their spiritual journey and in their daily lives.
